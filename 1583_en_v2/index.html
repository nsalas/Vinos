1583: Albariño de Fefiñales

1583 was the year in which the original builder of the Palacio de Fefiñanes was born. It was our wish to pay homepage to our forebear with a wine that would marry together, in perfect harmony, the innate virtues of the Albariño grape with the complex overtones of oak.

"When you open a bottle of 1583 sparks fly, good vibes flowing, electricity ... Do you feel like? You have to been ready for a different taste and play the wine!"

© Winery: Palacio de Fefiñanes, 2007

Palacio de Fefiñanes

The Palacio de Fefiñanes has been linked to wine since the 17th century. And it was a hundred years ago, that the winery was created as a business venture.

In 1928, the trademark and label Albariño de Fefiñanes was created, becoming a real pioneer.

The outstanding Albariño grape from the Salnés Valley has always been the main protagonist of our wines. It's a variety which we have nurtered to maintain its character, extraordinary expressiveness and uniqueness.

Tasting notes

Home

Appearance

The glass has very soft straw color with slightly greenish hue, if compared it with Fefiñanes 2006 (no wood) the color is much more straw than gold and more relaxed.

Nose

The nose also provides us with fresh, floral notes, a very light touch roasted (burnt sugar), white fruit but also appears more mature and not so fresh, it also has hints of herbal freshness and creaminess (touch of pastry), appear new aspects of citrus and lime.

Taste

The palate is very silky, more unctuous than the last, are notes of exotic fruits even above those of white fruit, incredibly balanced acidity, creamy and citrus again.
