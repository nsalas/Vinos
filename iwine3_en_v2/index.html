iWine

Home

iWine is made with Albariño grapes and dry ice maceration at very low temperatures to create a unique wine of intense aromas and potent flavors.

"When you open a bottle of iWine sparks fly and the beat flows...
Are you up for it? Prepare for something different, open the bottle and fill your glass, turn up the volume and play the wine!"

Description

Home

Color

Blissful, clean and brilliant yellow straw color with green highlights dancing in the light.

Aroma

Rhythmic, delicate and intense. Harmonious and full of personality with notes of mature fruit, fresh flowers and smooth balsamic.

Taste

Melodic, fresh, acidic and alive. Balanced and full elegant notes and deep flavors than linger.

Tasting notes

Home

Visual phase

Pale straw yellow with greenish hues, very clean and bright.

Nose phase

Aromas of good intensity and finesse, ripe white fruit (apple, pineapple). Well wrapped by fresh floral (acacia) and delicate balsamic overtones. A harmonious whole that defines the character of the variety.

Taste phase

The palate is rich, meaty, with a lively, fresh acidity, well balanced, full of aromatic nuances of great finesse and elegance with an aftertaste where stands a structure that gives length and depth.
